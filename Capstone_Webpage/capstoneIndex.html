About Us:

Sponsored by a Seattle food experience startup, Cooktour, our team set out to solve a common problem shared amongst startups: unsuccessful Facebook ad campaigns attributted to mistargeted ads and irrelevant content. Our solution is a dashboard and workflow that integrates machine learning to predict the results of Facebook Ads for traffic, engagement and conversion.
